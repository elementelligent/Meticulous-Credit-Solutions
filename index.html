What You Can Do To Improve Your Credit Score

  • Tip #1: Pay Your Bills on Time Moving forward.
  • Tip #2: Pay More Than the Minimum Amount Due.
  • Tip #3: Keep your Balances Low.
  • Tip #4: Write out a Budget.
  • Tip #5: Work on Paying Off Debt.
  • Tip #6: Don’t Close Old Accounts, Even if You Are Not Currently Using Them.
  • Tip #7: Avoid Applying for an Abundance of New Credit.
  • Tip #8: Become an Authorized User on Someone’s Account.
